ShelfApp

This department is responsible for the further development of an app which is used internally in our store.

The shelf app enables the employees in the store to scan each item or the price tag on site. This scan shows the current stock of the item and where it is and where this item is located in the store. If it is out of stock, the current delivery time is displayed. Through this function, the employees in the store have the possibility to tell the customer who is interested in an item if it is in stock or how soon it could be delivered. could be delivered. Should he be on site, it is possible through the location to get the article without having to search for it. This app simplifies the everyday life of our own employees and improves the shopping experience of our customers.

Assortment and Space Management

Provides a website with information about the sales information of individual products and product groups for each shop from Germany. We work in teams and use Scrum for agile working. The team is split into frontend, backend and data analysts.

The frontend team takes care of creating the website with react and designs the user interface with figma. The backend team is providing the data from Google Cloud to the website, additionally they are programming scripts to export the data from csv files to Google Cloud databases. The data analysts analyze the data e.g. for sales increases of individual products or how to use the space in the markets as effective as possible, moreover they create SQL querys.
